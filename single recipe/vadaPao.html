Vada Pav

Vada Pao is one of the most loves food across India. Vada made of spices and potatoes sandwiched between toasted pao bread, this dish is a filling meal that you can have for a light lunch or brunch or as an evening snacks.

Perp Time: 10 Minute | Cock Time: 1 hrs

Step of Recipe

Step 1:-

Make the garlic coconut chutney

Step 2:-

Make the green chutney

Step 3:-

Make the tamarind chutney

Step 4:-

Make the potato balls aka the vada

Step 5:-

Make the batter to dip the vadas

Step 6:-

Fry the vada in hot oil

Step 7:-

Heat up the pav (buns)

Step 8:-

And finally assemble it all together!

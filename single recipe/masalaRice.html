Masala Rice

Masala Fried Rice is a simple and easy-to-make Chinese recipe. The main ingredients used to make this quick and lip-smacking dish are rice, mixed veggies and spices. This Chinese rice dish is quite similar to Indian veg-biryani recipe. Masala Fried Rice can be prepared at brunch, lunch or dinner as a main dish. You can serve it on get-together along with pickle, raita or a bowl of yogurt, according to your choice. This delicious rice recipe would be cherished by adults and kids alike. Serve it to your family and friends

Perp Time: 10 Minute | Cock Time: 30 Minute

Step of Recipe

Step 1:-

wash and soak the rice in water for at least 10minutes.Here I soaked the rice for 20 minutes.

Step 2:-

Heat a deep pan with ghee and oil. Then add all spices and fry for a minute.

Step 3:-

Next, add the chopped onions along with slit green chillies and sauté them till onion changes its colour.

Step 4:-

After that add ginger garlic paste and fry a minute or until raw smell goes out.

Step 5:-

Then add chopped tomatoes along with turmeric powder, coriander leaves, mint leaves and sauté them for a minute.

Step 6:-

Cover and cook till the tomatoes are nicely mashed (don’t forget to stir in between otherwise masala will stick to the bottom of the pan).

Step 7:-

Drain the water from rice and slowly add the pan and mix gently, so that you don’t break the grains.

Step 8:-

Add 11/2 cup of water and season it with required salt and mix well.

Step 9:-

Close the lid and cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes or until rice is cooked.

Step 10:-

Transfer the flavoured masala rice into a serving plate and enjoy with favourite curry or with raita.
